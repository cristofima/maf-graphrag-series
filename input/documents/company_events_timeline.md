# TechVenture Inc. - Company Events & Timeline

## Company Milestones

### 2022 - Foundation Year

**January 2022 - Company Founded**

- Initial vision: Enterprise AI solutions leveraging knowledge graphs
- Seed funding: $2.5M from angel investors

**March 2022 - First Hires**
- Dr. Emily Harrison joined as VP of AI Research
- Jennifer Park joined as Infrastructure Lead
- Initial team of 5 employees

**June 2022 - Series A**

**September 2022 - First Product Concept**
- Dr. Emily Harrison proposed GraphRAG-powered enterprise assistant
- Proof of concept demo to leadership
- Decision to pursue as flagship product
