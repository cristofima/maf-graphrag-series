## Customer Success Stories

### Case Study: GlobalBank Fraud Detection

**Challenge:** GlobalBank processed 10 million transactions daily with a 2% false positive rate in fraud detection, costing $5M annually in manual review.

**Solution:** TechVenture deployed an AI-powered analytics platform with:
- Real-time transaction scoring (Priya Patel's architecture)
- Knowledge graph for relationship detection (Sophia Lee's GraphRAG)
- Custom LLM for explanation generation (Dr. James Mitchell)

**Results:**
- 60% reduction in false positives
- $3M annual cost savings
- 99.2% fraud detection accuracy
- 200ms average decision latency

**Quote:** "TechVenture's AI platform transformed our fraud operations. The explanation capability is game-changing for our compliance team." - Margaret Wilson, CTO

### Case Study: Meridian Healthcare Readmission Prediction

**Challenge:** Meridian's hospitals faced 18% 30-day readmission rate, above the national average and impacting reimbursements.

**Solution:** Project Beta deployment with:
- Predictive model for readmission risk (Elena Rodriguez)
- Clinical NLP for discharge notes (Alex Turner)
- Care coordination dashboard (Carlos Martinez design)

**Results:**
- 12% readmission rate (reduction of 6 percentage points)
- $2.5M avoided penalties
- 85% clinician adoption rate
- Real-time risk scoring at discharge
